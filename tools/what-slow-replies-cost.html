When a new lead comes in, the first one to reply usually wins the job. Reply slow and the money walks out the door.

Why this matters: people who ask about your work are ready to buy now. Make them wait and they go cold, call someone else, and shop on price. Fast replies win more jobs for the same number of leads.

Quick wins — start here

  • Answer every new lead within 60 seconds. Even a quick "got it, calling you now" keeps them warm.
  • Call first, then text or email. A call wins more jobs than a message.
  • Follow up at least twice. Most jobs are lost because nobody followed up.
  • Have someone or something ready to answer after hours and while you are on the job.
